Bot Commands

Here are all the commands available for the Warzone Team Finder bot.

/registrar

Register your Activision ID and K/D ratio to join teams.

How to use:
/registrar

This will open a registration form where you can enter your Activision ID and K/D ratio.

/perfil

View your registered profile information.

How to use:
/perfil

Displays your registered Activision ID and K/D ratio.

/buscar_equipo

Create a team search for others to join.

How to use:
/buscar_equipo plataforma: [PC/Xbox/PlayStation/Crossplay] modo: [Battle Royale/Resurgimiento/etc] kd_minimo: [number] max_jugadores: [2/3/4] descripcion: [optional text]
Parameters:
  • plataforma: Your gaming platform (PC, Xbox, PlayStation, or Crossplay)
  • modo: Game mode (Battle Royale, Resurgimiento, Ranked BR, etc)
  • kd_minimo: Minimum K/D ratio required (default: 0.0)
  • max_jugadores: Maximum team size (Duo=2, Trio=3, Squad=4)
  • descripcion: Optional description for your team search

  • Voice Channel Detection: If you're in a voice channel when creating a team, it will be displayed in the search
