About Warzone Team Finder

Our Mission

Warzone Team Finder is a Discord bot designed to help Warzone players find teammates based on platform, game mode, and skill level. Our mission is to make it easy for players to connect with like-minded gamers to enhance their Warzone experience.

Key Features

  • ✅ Registration of Activision IDs and KD ratio
  • ✅ Team search with platform and game mode filtering
  • ✅ Interactive team joining with buttons
  • ✅ User profiles with stats
  • ✅ Customizable team sizes (Duo, Trio, Squad)
  • ✅ KD minimum requirements

How It Works

Warzone Team Finder uses Discord's slash command system to provide a seamless experience for users. Players can register their Activision ID and K/D ratio, then create or join team searches based on specific criteria such as platform, game mode, and skill level.

Privacy

We only store the information you provide when using our bot commands. This includes your Discord ID, Activision ID, and K/D ratio. This information is only used to help match you with appropriate teammates.
